ARPANET and the Beginning of the Internet

You most likely use the internet everyday and not think twice about it. Or you may think that the internet is something magical that people smarter than yourself thought up in a dream and put that dream to reality. Whatever you think about the internet (unless you already the contents of this webpage) is not entirely true. The people who made the internet are most likely smarter than you and me, but that does not stop you from learning the conditions for the internet to be made, the steps it took the reserachers to make it, and how it eventually started to resemble what it currently is.
